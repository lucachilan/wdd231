Site Plan: LingoFlip

A website planning document by Luca Chilan

Site Name

LingoFlip

Reason: The name is highly engaging and direct. "Lingo" connects instantly with language and vocabulary, while "Flip" refers to the core action of turning over a flashcard. It sounds like a modern, interactive learning tool.
Optional domain availability: lingoflip-app.com

Site Purpose

LingoFlip is a vocabulary flashcard application built for language learners. By connecting to a public dictionary API, it fetches definitions, examples, and pronunciation aids. The app divides the experience into learning new cards, reviewing a "learned" collection, and a dedicated practice area, solving the everyday needs of language teachers and their students.

Scenarios

  • How can I quickly pull up flashcards to learn 5 new words today?
  • Where do I review and practice the specific vocabulary words I struggled with yesterday?
  • How can I access my history list of "learned" words to know my progress?

Color Scheme

The site employs a modern, clean, and vibrant palette to keep learners focused and engaged without harsh distractions.

Primary: #4F46E5

Deep Indigo. Used for headers, cards, and primary structural elements.

Secondary: #06B6D4

Vibrant Teal. Used for interactive progress and success highlights.

Background: #F8FAFC

Soft Slate White. Main body background for a crisp, readable UI.

Text: #1E293B

Dark Slate. Primary text color for excellent legibility.

Accent: #F59E0B

Amber. High-contrast accent color for buttons, alerts, and interactions.

Typography

We use Google Fonts to bring modern, legible typography to the site:

  • Headings: 'Outfit', sans-serif - A modern, geometric sans-serif that looks friendly and confident.
  • Body: 'Inter', sans-serif - The industry standard for UI reading. Highly legible for reading flashcard definitions.
