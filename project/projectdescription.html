Site Plan: Adventure Awaits

A website planning document by Luca Chilan

Site Name

Adventure Awaits

Reason: This name actively encourages adventure and appeals to thrill-seekers looking to book outdoor activities like rafting, hiking, and guided climbing. It creates a sense of excitement and immediate action.
Optional domain availability: adventure-awaits-booking.com

Site Purpose

The site offers booking services for guided outdoor adventures and provides detailed localized information about each trip, including difficulty, duration, and what to pack. It aims to connect adventurers with knowledgeable guides to explore the outdoors safely.

Scenarios

  • What kind of gear do I need for the multi-day white water rafting trip?
  • How do I book a group hiking tour for my family on a weekend?
  • What is the cancellation policy if the weather is too dangerous for our planned climbing trip?

Color Scheme

The site employs an earthy, nature-inspired palette to reflect the outdoor theme.

Primary: #2F4F4F

Used for headers, footers, and major UI sections.

Secondary: #8B4513

Used for accents, borders, and main call-to-action buttons.

Background: #F5F5DC

Main body background for a warm, readable contrast.

Text: #333333

Primary text color for readability.

Accent: #4682B4

Accent color for links and hover interactions.

Typography

We use Google Fonts to bring modern, legible typography to the site:

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - Used for modern, clean, and bold titles.
  • Body: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Selected for its excellent readability in paragraph text and list items.
