Non Profit Membership Level

Cost: Free

Benefits: Access to monthly networking events, inclusion in our online directory, and opportunities to volunteer at community events.

Bronze Membership Level

Cost: $50 / year

Benefits: All Non Profit benefits plus reduced rates for special training sessions, and access to members-only newsletter.

Silver Membership Level

Cost: $100 / year

Benefits: All Bronze benefits plus event discounts, advertising in our monthly newsletter, and up to 2 spotlight positions per year.

Gold Membership Level

Cost: $250 / year

Benefits: All Silver benefits plus VIP access to all special events, 5 spotlight positions per year on the home page, and full training packages.
